Game Overview

Unleash your inner artist in the heat of competition with Pigment Wars!
This 3D multiplayer casual game transforms painting into a thrilling showdown for color dominance, where every roll of your brush leaves a vibrant mark. Take control of a quirky bean-like figure and dive into fast-paced matches across dynamic arenas—from floating tiles to spinning cubes—each one a canvas filled with creative chaos. Whether you’re a precise path painter or a bold territory roller, there’s a place for every playstyle. Cover the arena in your team’s color, outpaint your rivals, and prove your dominance. Will your color reign supreme?
