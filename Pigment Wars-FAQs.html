Frequently Asked Questions

To answer your most frequent questions, we present these answers with great anticipation for your gameplay! Enjoy!

Pigment Wars is a 3D multiplayer casual game where players compete to cover the most area of a floating arena using their paintbrushes while maintaining balance and strategy.

Pigment Wars is being developed for PC, with controls optimized for keyboard input (WASD for movement, Space for jumping). It's the only platform it is available on for now.

The game supports up to 2 players, either playing individually or in teams.

The game is designed for players aged 10 and up, featuring accessible gameplay and colorful graphics without violence.

Pigment Wars offers 3 unique maps with increasing difficulty—ranging from floating tiles to complex environment—designed to test players' strategy through obstacles and balance.

Multiplayer functionality is powered by Netcode for GameObjects, allowing real-time interaction between players in a shared arena.

Players choose a color, move using WASD keys, jump with Spacebar, and compete to paint as many tiles as possible. Strategic movement and timing are essential due to limited balance and the presence of in-game hazards.

The game includes applied physics like gravity, friction, and collisions. AI features include random power-up spawns and a Color Sweeper robot that erases painted areas, adding challenge and unpredictability.

Power-ups spawn at random intervals and locations, providing boosts or advantages that help players paint faster, move quicker, or hinder opponents.

While the game is primarily focused on entertainment, it subtly incorporates applied game physics, AI, and mathematical logic like timers—making it a fun way to experience foundational game development concepts in action.
