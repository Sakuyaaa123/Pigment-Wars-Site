Game Features

WHAT MAKES IT STANDOUT?

Exciting 3D Multiplayer Battles

Jump into real-time battles with players around the world! Engage in fast-paced paint fights where strategy, speed, and creativity lead to victory.

Dynamic Arenas with Floating Platforms

Battle on unique floating environments that keep changing the way you play. From shifting tiles to rotating cubes, every match feels fresh and unpredictable.

Game Mechanics

PIGMENT WARS is a thrilling 2-player game that can be played solo or in pairs. Once a game mode is selected, players are paired and prompted to choose their unique paint colors—no duplicates allowed, as the system ensures color selections are distinct. Players then dive into a futuristic, floating arena where balance is key. One wrong step and they’ll fall, triggering a respawn penalty. Strategic color placement, movement precision, and map awareness are crucial as each player aims to out-paint and outmaneuver their opponent on a dynamic battleground.

Objectives

In Pigment Wars, players must strategically claim and maintain control over as many tiles as possible using their chosen paint color, all while battling the clock and each other on a thrilling floating arena. Victory goes to the player with the most painted tiles by the end of the match—but staying on top isn't easy. Players must dodge "Freeze" traps, watch out for the unpredictable Pigment Eraser AI that wipes clean everything in its path, and navigate a variety of dynamic environmental obstacles. Fall off the arena and you’ll face a respawn delay, giving your opponent time to steal your progress. On difficult mode, the pressure intensifies—even lingering on the same tile for more than a few seconds will cause an automatic fall. Precision, awareness, and quick thinking are key to becoming the ultimate pigment master!

Map Selection & Difficulty

The game features 3 unique maps, each with increasing difficulty to challenge players of different skill levels.

The Cityscape (Easy Mode)

This beginner-friendly map features a moderately tilting floating arena with square tiles. It's set against a bright urban skyline. Obstacles include rising walls and freeze traps. Perfect for newcomers to learn mechanics without overwhelming hazards.

  • Obstacle: Wall Up
  • Obstacle: Freeze
  • Tilting Intensity: Mild

The Starscape (Difficult Mode)

Mid-level challenge with a sleek hexagonal tile layout. The arena tilts more frequently, adding risk to every move. Drop holes appear randomly, alongside the existing freeze and wall obstacles.

  • Obstacle: Wall Up
  • Obstacle: Freeze
  • Obstacle: Drop Holes
  • Tilting Intensity: Medium

The Sunscape (Average Mode)

The ultimate test of skill. A deep-space-inspired arena with the most extreme tilting and the highest number of hazards. Disappearing tiles punish slow players, and drop holes create intense pressure. Only the best can master this map.

  • Obstacle: Wall Up
  • Obstacle: Freeze
  • Obstacle: Drop Holes
  • Obstacle: Disappearing Tiles
  • Tilting Intensity: High

Controls & Physics

  • Movement: WASD keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Collisions: With other players, power-ups, monsters, and arena boundaries
  • Gravity: Players can fall off edges
  • Friction: Affects movement after dashing

Power-Ups

Power-ups randomly spawn within the arena and can change the tide of battle. Their spawn locations and timing are unpredictable, so keep an eye out!

Speed Up – Boosts the player's movement speed, allowing quicker navigation across the arena. Lasts for 5 seconds.

Paint Expansion – Expands the player’s paint coverage to a 3x3 tile area, letting them color more tiles in one move. Lasts for 5 seconds.

Tile Shield – Prevents the player’s painted tiles from being overwritten, keeping their area protected. Lasts for 5 seconds.

Slow Down – Temporarily reduces the opponent’s speed by half, slowing their ability to move and paint. Lasts for 5 seconds.

Pigment Eraser (Color Sweeper AI)

Pigment Erasers are robot-like AI enemies that randomly appear and roam around the arena. Their purpose? To erase any painted tiles they pass over—wiping away your hard-earned territory. These unpredictable machines follow no fixed path, keeping players on their toes. Touching a Pigment Eraser doesn't just stop your progress—it repels and bounces you back, making it harder to maintain your balance and control in the floating arena. Stay alert and outmaneuver them to protect your painted zone!

Time Limit

Each match lasts for 1 minute, pushing players to act fast, think smart, and paint more before the time runs out.

Obstacles

The arena features freeze traps, shifting tiles, and moving walls that challenge player movement. Stepping on a freeze trap stops you temporarily, while dynamic platforms can block paths or push players off. On harder maps, standing still for too long can cause instant falls—so stay on the move!

Freeze

Must be avoided. If collected, the player becomes frozen for 4 seconds.

Wall Up

A sudden wall rises in the arena. Appears in Easy mode.

Drop Hole

Open holes in the arena that players can fall through. First appears in Average mode.

Disappearing Tiles

Activated in Difficult mode. Players must not stay on the same tile for more than 2 seconds, or they will fall.
